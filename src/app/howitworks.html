How it works

Qualifying for Hospice

To qualify for hospice, patients must have a life-limiting illness and no longer respond to or seek curative treatment.  Hospice is designed to comfort both the patient and entire family and improve the quality of days ahead for everyone. Please reach out if you have any questions regarding eligibility and we will work through the process with you.

Who pays for hospice?

Medicare covers hospice at 100 percent. This includes the services of the Interdisciplinary team- RN, Social Work, Chaplain, Aide as well as DME, Medications and Supplies. what to expect. Medicaid and private insurance sometimes covers hospice as well, and there are options for unfunded patients to receive care. Please reach out to our team if you have a question about funding.

What to Expect

Hospice is patient centered and focused on providing the best possible care at a very challenging stage in life. We offer 24/7 on call, Aide assistance for bathing and dressing, in-home nursing with a team of RN’s and LVN’s, chaplain support, social worker support, and volunteers. We walk our patients and their families through every step of this difficult process, ensuring they are comfortable with the hospice philosophy and happy with the care we provide.
